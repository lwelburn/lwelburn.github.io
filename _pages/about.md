I'm a mechanical engineering PhD Research Assistant with a background in anatomy/biomechanics and my research focuses on dexterous robotic hand manipulation, reinforcement learning, and control theory. My current project, **ReAct Hand** (Remote Actuation Hand), explores tendon-driven finger actuation using MuJoCo simulation and moteus-based hardware, with a goal of Sim2Real transfer for industrial manipulation tasks.
